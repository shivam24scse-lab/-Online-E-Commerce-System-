## üìä Presentation Structure Overview

Your presentation tells this story:

```
1. Title ‚Üí Introduction
2. Problem ‚Üí What challenges exist?
3. Solution ‚Üí How does our system solve them?
4-6. Features ‚Üí What can users and admins do?
7. Architecture ‚Üí How is it built?
8-9. Benefits ‚Üí Why is this valuable?
10. Workflow ‚Üí How does it work?
11. Advantages ‚Üí Why choose us?
12. Future ‚Üí What's next?
13. Conclusion ‚Üí Summary of impact
14. Thank You ‚Üí Questions and contact
```

**Total Time**: 15-20 minutes + Q&A

## üé® Design Quick Tips

### Colors (Copy these hex codes):
- **Primary Blue**: #0066CC (headers, important text)
- **Success Green**: #00A651 (positive points)
- **Professional Gray**: #4D4D4D (body text)
- **Accent Orange**: #FF6B35 (call-to-action)
- **Light Blue**: #4DA6FF (backgrounds)

### Fonts:
- **Headers**: Arial Bold, 32-44pt
- **Body**: Arial Regular, 18-24pt
- **Small text**: Arial Regular, 14-16pt

### Layout Rules:
- **Maximum 6 bullet points** per slide
- **Maximum 6-8 words** per bullet point
- **One main idea** per slide
- **Plenty of white space** for readability

## üí° Common Questions

### Q: How long will this take to present?
**A:** 15-20 minutes for slides + 5-10 minutes for Q&A = 20-30 minutes total

### Q: Can I skip slides?
**A:** Yes! For shorter presentations, keep slides 1-3, 5-6, 8-9, 13-14 (core message)

### Q: What if I don't have PowerPoint?
**A:** Use Google Slides (free) or any presentation software. The content works in any format.

### Q: Do I need to use all the technical details?
**A:** No! Adjust technical depth based on your audience. Focus more on benefits for business audiences.

### Q: Can I add my own slides?
**A:** Absolutely! These are templates. Add case studies, team info, pricing, or anything relevant.

### Q: How do I handle questions I can't answer?
**A:** Refer to PROJECT_OVERVIEW.md for details, or offer to follow up after reviewing documentation.
